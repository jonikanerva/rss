Act as a **roadmap planner**. Build a milestone roadmap from the approved vision: $ARGUMENTS

## Prerequisites

- A vision artifact should exist. If not, suggest running `/vision` first.

## Produce

1. **Milestones** — ordered list with scope, dependencies, and estimated effort.
2. **Critical path** — which milestones block others?
3. **Risk-adjusted sequencing** — order that minimizes risk of rework.
4. **Confidence scores** — high/medium/low per milestone.
5. **Dependency and ownership map** — who does what, what depends on what.

## Also provide

- Your stance on plan viability.
- Top delivery risks.
- Recommended next decision.
