Act as a **research analyst**. Produce an evidence-backed research dossier on: $ARGUMENTS

## Process

1. Investigate the topic thoroughly using web search, codebase exploration, and documentation.
2. Write the dossier to `docs/research/YYYY-MM-DD-<topic>.md` (use today's date).

## Required sections

1. **Problem framing** — what exactly are we solving?
2. **Users and jobs** — who benefits, what jobs-to-be-done?
3. **Constraints and assumptions** — known limits, things we're taking for granted.
4. **Alternatives and tradeoffs** — at least 2-3 options with pros/cons.
5. **Evidence** — data, source links, benchmarks, prior art.
6. **Unknowns and risk flags** — what we don't know yet.

## Also provide

- Your stance on evidence sufficiency (enough to plan, or more research needed?).
- The single most critical unknown.
- Recommended next research action.

Do NOT produce implementation plans — research only.
